Sands of Journey


In this little world inspired by Journey, Sable the game and Star Wars, the small orange beings were left behind with the hope of a purpose greater than all the riches and technologies of the earth.

Through low-poly modeling and simplistic textures, I wanted to communicate the richness of the earth and the great architectural achievements in contrast to the simple clothes of the orange beings and mounds of unpolished stones on the ground.

By looking at inspirations from other works on the internet and seeing topologies here and there, I'm trying to improve my understanding of topology and how it can relate to bone structures for animation using blender 3D software

DisasterPiece


All these assets were created to integrate into the Unreal Engine and develop the game Disasterpiece.

Disasterpiece is a 3D physics-based game where two thieves steal a set of items scattered across the map, but cannot trigger alarms or call the police. The objective is to steal as many items as possible without being caught.

My role in the video game was to model and animate objects that could fill this entire context. I created simple everyday objects, modelable objects (to assemble the scenery like Lego bricks), vehicles, and characters with basic animations.

EMtY


EMtY (inspired by characters from the games Journey and Hollow Knight) was the first character I created and developed a bone structure for animation. It was the first step in developing my animation skills in Blender software.
